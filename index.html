Animal Sponsorship & Adoption

Adopting an animal establishes a direct sponsorship. You receive a certification plaque, monthly health updates, and camera access to their habitat.

How to Adopt:

  1. Go to the Animal Directory.
  2. Search or select a species (e.g. Reticulated Giraffe).
  3. Open their profile to view the individual animal listings.
  4. Click the Adopt button next to a specific named individual (e.g. "Melman").

Certified Supporter

All sponsorships are simulated for educational demonstration. Generating an adoption pass will download a virtual certificate frame containing your animal details!
